### BMI Categories:
## Underweight =< 18.5
## Normal weight = 18.5 <–> 24.9
## Overweight = 25 <–> 29.9
## Obesity = BMI of 30 or greater
##
## Calculation: BMI = kg/m2 where kg is a person's weight in kilograms and m2 is their height in metres squared
## Example: weight = 81, height = 179cm, BMI = 32.05
